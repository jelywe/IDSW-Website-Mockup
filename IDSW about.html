The Infectious Diseases Society of Washington is a 501c(6) organization registered in the state of Washington.

Mission Statement

IDSW brings together infectious diseases clinicians and partners across Washington State to strengthen patient care, support the ID workforce, and advance evidence-based public health practice.

Our mission is to foster a connected community of ID professionals who collaborate on clinical excellence, stewardship, education, and advocacy—so that every Washington resident has access to high-quality infectious diseases expertise.

  • Connect ID professionals across the state
  • Promote antimicrobial stewardship and best practices
  • Support education, mentorship, and training
  • Advance policy that protects patients and communities

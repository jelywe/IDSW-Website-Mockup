Membership

Join IDSW to connect with infectious diseases professionals across Washington State, support education and collaboration, and strengthen the collective voice of ID in our region.

Benefits of Membership

IDSW membership supports a statewide professional community dedicated to clinical excellence, stewardship, education, and advocacy.

  • Connect with colleagues for shared patients, referrals, and collaboration
  • Access member updates, educational opportunities, and events
  • Contribute to policy and advocacy efforts that protect patients and communities
  • Share stories and learn from peers across diverse practice settings
  • Support stewardship, workforce development, and training pathways in Washington
